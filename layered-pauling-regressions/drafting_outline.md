# Draft Notes for the Electronegativity Discussion

These points are intended as a starting draft for a longer write-up.

## Main observations

- oxygen `min delta_E` shows the clearest positive trend with metal electronegativity
- sulfur `max delta_E` shows the clearest negative trend with metal electronegativity
- mean and median trends are weak for both ligands
- the extreme statistics carry more visible signal than the central statistics

## Suggested discussion points

- electronegativity does not provide a single universal explanation for binary `delta_E`
- oxygen and sulfur do not follow the same ordering of trends
- outliers still matter, especially in the `min delta_E` panels
- the cleaner multi-regression grid is more useful for discussion than the original single-statistic figure set
